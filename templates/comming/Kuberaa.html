Kuberaa

"Kuberaa" is an upcoming Indian social thriller film directed by Sekhar Kammula, starring Dhanush, Nagarjuna, Rashmika Mandanna, Jim Sarbh, and Dalip Tahil. It is simultaneously shot in Telugu, Tamil, and Hindi and is scheduled to be released theatrically worldwide on June 20, 2025. The film is produced by Suniel Narang and Puskur Rammohan Rao under Amigos Creations. The film's music is composed by the renowned M. M. Keeravani, known for his work in the "Baahubali" series and "RRR". The cinematography is handled by Vijay C. Kumar, while the editing is done by Marthand K. Venkatesh. The film's story revolves around the pursuit of wealth and its consequences, exploring themes of greed, ambition, and moral dilemmas faced by the characters leading to a quest for redemption.
