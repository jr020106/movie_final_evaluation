Housefull 5

Housefull 5 is an upcoming Indian comedy film co-written and directed by Tarun Mansukhani and produced by Sajid Nadiadwala under his production banner, Nadiadwala Grandson Entertainment. It marks the fifth installment of the Housefull franchise. The film features an ensemble cast including Akshay Kumar, Riteish Deshmukh, Abhishek Bachchan, Sanjay Dutt, Fardeen Khan, Shreyas Talpade, Nana Patekar, Jackie Shroff, Dino Morea, Jacqueline Fernandez, Nargis Fakhri, Chitrangada Singh, Sonam Bajwa, Soundarya Sharma, Chunky Pandey and Johnny Lever.
