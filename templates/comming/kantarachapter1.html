Kantara A Legend: Chapter 1

"Kantara: Chapter 1" is a prequel to the 2022 Kannada film "Kantara" directed by Rishab Shetty. It delves into the origins of the deities Panjurli Daiva and Guliga Daiva, drawing inspiration from ancient Kadamba period mythology and history. The film is set during the Kadamba dynasty in Karnataka and explores the rich cultural and historical significance of the era. It features a massive war sequence choreographed by both national and international experts, with over 500 fighters and a cast of nearly 3,000 people.
