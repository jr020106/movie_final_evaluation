Our Story

MovieFlix was born from a late-night conversation among friends who loved cinema. We realized that while there are many platforms, none felt truly made for us. What started as a fun coding experiment turned into a full-fledged movie site. We spent days designing, coding, and debating every feature. With passion and countless cups of chai, MovieFlix slowly came to life. Today, it's more than just a site — it’s our tribute to the world of movies
